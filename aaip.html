What is the Alberta Advantage Immigration Program (AAIP)?

The Alberta Advantage Immigration Program (AAIP) was formerly known as the Alberta Immigrant Nominee Program (AINP). This is a provincial nominee program (PNP) exclusive to Alberta. The purpose of the program is to attract skilled immigrants who can establish or purchase a business in Alberta or who can cover labor shortages in the region. You may apply for permanent residence together with your spouse or common-law partner and your dependent children if you are chosen. The initiative is overseen by the province of Alberta and the federal government of Canada.
